Easy As Pie

Pies are not easy to buy, but they are easy to eat. Especially when the pies are made by places like this month's Tired Cliche Food Court partner, Ted's Bakery.

This increasingly-popular bakery on Oahu's North Shore creates pies with local flavors, such as macadamia nuts and haupia (a traditional Hawaiian dessert made from coconut milk).

They are also known for seasonal specialties, such as Pumpkin Haupia Pie.

Visit their website , especially their bakery page.

Mention us while you're there, and you'll be kicked out of the store you'll get a 25% discount off all pie purchases!

Bringing Home The Bacon

Ok, it's hard to find a restaurant that serves only bacon, but it (among many other things) is on the menu at this month's Tired Cliche Food Court partner, The Curious Pig

There is a certain horror-film aura to a meat restaurant being named The Curious Pig - "No, little buddy, don't go in there!" But the quality of the food and the crowds of customers have gained this restaurant a spot in Michigan news website MLive's Ten Best BBQ Restaurants in Michigan list.

Not bad, for a restaurant located waaaaaaaay up yonder in the center-western reaches of Michigan's Upper Peninsula.

Mention us while you're there, and they'll make you help wash dishes you'll get a 25% discount off of anything containing bacon!

An Apple A Day...

This might be a bit of a stretch, but stick with us here. Apples are great to eat, but they can be even better to drink.

We feel that apple juice is good, but apple cider is better. And while apple cider is a winner, sometimes you might want a, um, more adult version - one with an alcohol percentage attached.

This brings us to yet another of this month's Tired Cliche Food Court partner, The Cinder Block Brewery.

(Note: the website asks if you are 21 or older before proceeding.)

One of the more popular local breweries in the Kansas City area, Cinder Block Brewery's beer page features hard cider as well, including French Cider and English Cherry Cider.

If you're in the Kansas City area, and have alternate arrangements for any under-21 members of your travelling party, mention us while you're there. You'll find that if you mention us, they'll throw stale marshmallows at you they'll offer you a 25% discount off of either of their hard ciders.
