You can change the colors of the box with your very own mouse or trackpad!

  • The box turns blue if you hover the mouse over the box.
  • The box turns red if you click down on the box with your mouse.
  • The box turns yellow if you release your click.
  • The box turns green if you double-click the box with your mouse.
  • The box turns orange if you scroll anywhere on the page.

You can also change colors by pressing a letter on the keyboard:

  • b for blue
  • r for red
  • y for yellow
  • g for green
  • o for orange
